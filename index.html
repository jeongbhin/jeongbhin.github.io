Interests

My research explores high-energy astrophysics and computational plasma physics, focusing on particle acceleration and transport across environments ranging from solar flares and the heliosphere to relativistic jets and galaxy clusters. I develop and apply advanced simulation frameworks—including WENO-based HD/MHD/RHD and the coupled MHD–SDE model—to study energetic-particle feedback in magnetic reconnection, shocks, and turbulence. Using these self-developed computational tools, I investigate particle acceleration mechanisms operating in diverse astrophysical plasma environments.

High Order WENO-based (HOW) Hydrodynamic Codes

HOW Hydrodynamic Codes are based on 5th order finite difference WENO and five stages of fourth-order SSPRK.
HD and MHD have two versions of the equation of state: ADI (adiabatic) and ISO (isothermal).
Please refer to our papers for details on these codes.
